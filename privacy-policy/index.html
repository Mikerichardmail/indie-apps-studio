Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

At Indie Apps Studio, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. Our applications are designed to respect your privacy and protect your personal data. This policy explains how we handle data across all our Android applications.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Our applications are designed to work completely offline and do not require internet access for core functionality.

The only data stored on your device is:

  • App settings and preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Scan history (if enabled, stored locally on your device)
  • Cache files for improved performance (stored locally on your device)

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use your data for any purpose. All data remains on your device and is under your complete control.

Data Sharing

We never share your data with third parties. We don't sell, rent, or share any user information because we don't collect it in the first place.

Analytics and Tracking

Our applications do not include any analytics, tracking, or monitoring software. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking services.

Advertising

Our applications are ad-free. We don't display advertisements or use any ad networks that could track your behavior across apps or websites.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security features. We recommend keeping your device updated and using secure lock screens.

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not collect any personal information, making them safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
