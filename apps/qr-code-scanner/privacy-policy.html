QrCode Scanner & Barcode Scan

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2024

Our Privacy Promise

QrCode Scanner & Barcode Scan is designed with your privacy as our top priority. We believe scanning QR codes and barcodes should not compromise your personal data.

Introduction

Welcome to QrCode Scanner & Barcode Scan ("we", "our", or "the app"). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user information and what data is (and is not) collected when you use our app.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Contacts

The app works without requiring user accounts or login.

2. Scanned Data (QR Codes & Barcodes)

When you scan a QR code or barcode:

  • The content is processed locally on your device
  • Scan results may be stored only on your device as scan history (if enabled)
  • Scan history can be cleared at any time by the user
  • Scan data is never uploaded to our servers
  • We do not access, monitor, or transmit scanned content

3. Payment & UPI QR Codes

If you scan a UPI payment QR code:

  • The app only reads the QR content
  • A preview is shown to the user
  • Payment is completed in third-party UPI apps (such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
  • We do not process payments
  • We do not store UPI IDs, amounts, or transaction details
  • All payment actions are handled entirely by external UPI applications

4. Scan History & Local Storage

If scan history is enabled:

  • History is stored locally on your device
  • Includes scanned content and timestamps
  • Can be deleted or disabled at any time
  • Never synced to cloud or servers

If Privacy Mode is enabled, no scan history is saved.

Permissions Used

The app requests only necessary permissions:

Camera Permission

  • Required to scan QR codes and barcodes
  • Used only when the scanner is active

Storage / Media Access (Optional)

  • Used to scan QR codes from gallery images
  • Used to save generated QR codes (if user chooses)

We do not use permissions for background tracking or data collection.

Advertisements

The free version of the app may show ads.

  • Ads are provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob)
  • Ad providers may collect anonymous, non-personal information to show relevant ads
  • This data is handled according to the ad provider's privacy policy
  • Users can remove ads by upgrading to the premium version

Third-Party Services

The app may interact with:

  • Web browsers (when opening links)
  • Email, phone, SMS apps (for actions chosen by the user)
  • UPI apps for payment processing
  • Ad networks (for displaying ads)

We do not control how third-party apps handle your data. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Data Security

We take privacy seriously:

  • No servers used for user data
  • No background data transmission
  • No selling or sharing of user data
  • Local device storage only

However, no method of storage is 100% secure. Users are advised to use device security features where available.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

User Control & Choices

You can:

  • Disable scan history
  • Clear stored data at any time
  • Enable Privacy Mode
  • Remove ads via premium upgrade
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
