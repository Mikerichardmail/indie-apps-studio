App Description

Experience the fastest and most secure QR code and barcode scanner available on Android. Our app works completely offline, ensuring your privacy is protected while delivering lightning-fast scanning performance. No ads, no tracking, no unnecessary permissions.

Key Features

  • Lightning Fast: Instant QR code and barcode recognition
  • 100% Offline: Works without internet connection
  • Privacy First: No data collection, no tracking
  • No Ads: Clean, uninterrupted experience
  • Scan History: Keep track of your scans
  • Multiple Formats: Supports all QR and barcode types
  • Auto-focus: Sharp and accurate scanning
  • Flash Support: Scan in low-light conditions
  • Share Results: Easily share scanned content
  • Copy to Clipboard: Quick data access

What Can You Scan?

QR Codes

  • Wi-Fi passwords

Barcodes
