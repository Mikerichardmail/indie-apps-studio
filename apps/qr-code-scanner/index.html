‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is this QR scanner really offline?

Yes! All QR code scanning, barcode reading, and QR generation work completely offline. Internet is only needed when you choose to open links or complete payments.

How does Safe Scan Mode work?

Safe Scan Mode shows you the content of QR codes before taking any action. You can preview URLs, text, or other content and decide whether to open, copy, share, or cancel.

Does this app support UPI payments?

Yes! The app fully supports UPI QR codes commonly used in India. It displays payment details clearly and redirects to your preferred UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.) for secure payment completion.

What types of barcodes can this scanner read?

The app supports all major barcode formats including UPC-A, UPC-E, EAN-8, EAN-13, Code 128, Code 39, Code 93, ITF, Codabar, and more.

Is my scan history private?

Absolutely! Scan history is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any servers. You can also enable Privacy Mode to disable history completely.
